TIME OF CRIME: 2:00 PM

SUSPECT: John "The Eel" Smith
RELATION: Former employee of the bank.

INTERROGATION TRANSCRIPT (2:00 PM, July 14th):

DETECTIVE: Where were you on Tuesday at 2:00 PM?
SUSPECT: I was miles away, officer. I was at the movies with my girlfriend.
DETECTIVE: Which movie?
